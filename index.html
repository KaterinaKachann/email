WEMAYHELP 2.0.

is a free mental counseling platform run by Ukrainian and Belarusian women. Professionals and volunteers in 12 time zones confidentially help those who are ready to accept help.

The project helps:

- victims of war in Ukraine and political persecution in Belarus and Russia;
- people traumatized because of what happened to their relatives;
- volunteers who help victims and traumatized people.

How to get help:

Everyone looking for help from psychologists, coaches and wellness and mindfulness teachers can get at least 3 free sessions. All you need to do is to text us via the Telegram messanger application.

The project origins:

The founder of WMH is Belarussian Nadia Damkovskaya, producer of international emotional projects. Nadia caught the outbreak of war in Kiev. WMH is the 15th charity project of the wellness platform HEALTH ME UP.
